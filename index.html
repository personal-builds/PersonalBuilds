Welcome to Personal Builds! This website is used for projecting your website ideas!

On Personal Builds we take in your information, and display it in a professional way allowing you, the user to conceptualize what your ideal professional personal website may look like. Please follow the instructions below:

  1. Click "Next" to get started!
  2. Fill in the title and the header appropriatly for your webpage
  3. Click "preview" to see how your header looks!
  4. Click on "choose an element" and pick an option that suits your needs
  5. Add your image in, or use one of the images we have supplied for you (image must be in URL form)
  6. add as many elements you want in any order to display your creativity!
  7. Click preview to refresh your webpage results
  8. Choose a color theme for your webpage
  9. once you have made the perfect website, you can click on "Get Code" to recieve the raw HTML and CSS that was used to create your webpage
  10. Please check out our Developers page!
  11. Thank you for using our website!
